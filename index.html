What we do

A full range of
cleaning services.

Whether it's your office, home, rental property, or a brand-new build — Ashburton Cleaning Services has you covered with tailored, professional cleaning every time.

Commercial Cleaning

General office cleaning, hard floor maintenance, and window cleaning. Customised schedules — after hours or during business — to suit your operation.

Domestic Cleaning

Individually tailored cleaning plans for your home. Weekly, fortnightly, monthly, or one-off — we work around your schedule and your priorities.

Window Cleaning

Professional streak-free window cleaning for commercial and domestic clients. We use industry methods that leave your glass spotless inside and out.

End of Tenancy

Comprehensive top-to-bottom cleaning designed to get your bond back. We cover everything from kitchen appliances to bathrooms — no shortcuts.

Builders Cleaning

Specialised post-construction cleaning for new homes and renovations — removing builder's dust, debris, and residue so your property is move-in ready.

Spring Cleaning

A thorough deep clean of all the hard-to-reach areas that regular cleaning misses. Perfect for a seasonal refresh that leaves your space looking brand new.
